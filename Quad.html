Quad Bike Safari Tours

  • Duration: 2 hours
  • Start at10:30 and 15:30am
  • Price per driver: €65 /Passengers: €25

Quad bike safaris are the best way to spice up your holiday by getting some excitement in a controlled and safe environment. This activity is suitable for all ages and is a great way to explore beyond the beach as we will take you into the interior of the beautiful island of Cyprus. Firstly we will demonstrate how to operate and safely ride our quad bikes then you will be provided with all the relevant safety equipment. You will start on flat tarmac surface then you will progress on to the dirt tracks where you will be led off through the traditional villages of Oroklini, Kellia, Troulli and the bi communal village of Pyla which is located on the buffer zone. On tour you will experience the wondrous untamed countryside like you have never seen before covering over 50km of various terrains through mountain trails, farm treks and country roads with stunning views and scenery that will take your breath away! Our trained staff will guide you on a 2+ hour awe-inspiring trip with 2 stops for a rest then continue through the hidden beauty and magnificent picturesque Cyprus which can only be achieved by a self-driven quad bike!

Important Notes

  • ✔ Please check-in for your tour at least 30 minutes prior to your tour time.
  • ✔ Drivers must be 18 years of age with a full driving license.

JEEP SAFARI

The ultimate off-road adventure for lovers of nature and EXTREME THRILLS. We start with a stop at one of the oldest monasteries on the island, nestled within the heart of Mosfiloti, a picturesque village in Larnaca’s district. The monastery is surrounded by a green-covered valley, offering a serene setting. From the IVth century till this day, holy water flows through the same spring and is believed to cure diseases.

Then, we visit the stunning red lake (copper mines) in the area, where you can walk around, study the rock formations, and collect precious stones. A magnificent image has been created! The reflection of the sun on the lake, combined with the remainder of the mines in the water, creates red and yellow colors, making for an impressive location.

We are then ready for a coffee stop at a traditional **Kafenio** in a tiny village among the local people, with a couple of pleasant surprises guaranteed for all guests. Then, the off-road adventure continues with a climb up the mountains for breath-taking views. Our journey continues to the **very private and enchanted Lagoon**, where you can take a tranquil swim.

The tour continues with a stop at a **museum village**, almost forgotten by time, where we really do take a step back into centuries gone by. **Lunch is optional** and can be enjoyed in the heart of this village in a typical **Cypriot family-owned Taverna**.

We continue our journey up the **mountain peaks**, stopping for photographs along the way. Our trip ends in one of the most beautiful and charming villages, **Lefkara**, which is often hailed as every traveler’s dream.

GHOST TOWN FAMAGUSTA

A Guided Tour into the Unknown

“Mini Adventure into the unknown of Ghost-town Famagusta”

  • Availability: Winter – 1st Dec to 28th Feb (Tuesdays)
  • Duration: 4.5 hours (plus journey time)
  • Hotel Pick-ups: Ayia Napa between 8:30am – 9:00am
  • Price: €50 Adults & €35 children

Included:

  • ✔ Pick up and drop off at the place of residence or agreed pick-up point.
  • ✔ Transportation (on- and off-road) in 4x4 Land Rover Defender with driver/guide.

Extras:

  • ✔ Refreshments along the way.

Join us on an in-depth view of **Famagusta** and the ongoing state of affairs with the **Ghost Town** and the illegal occupation of our Northern territories. Famagusta was once the **Key and the Heart of Cyprus**, the **hub of the Mediterranean**, where the spice routes met the silk roads. A place where **Greek, French, Armenian, Genoese, Venetian, Jewish, Syrian, Catalan, Pisan, Turkish, and Arab merchants** lived side by side, each in their own quarter, celebrating their **own traditions and religions**.

A city adorned with **365 churches**, one for every day of the year. A city that is now a **living museum**.

Our journey begins at the **Famagusta Cultural Centre**, where we will get a glimpse of the **abandoned city** from a short distance. Here, we will also watch a short film about Famagusta before the **1974 invasion** and its evacuation.

We then proceed to **cross the border** and drive along the **fenced-off** area, leading to the **Ghost City** itself. Here, you will have the unique opportunity to **walk along the golden-sandy beach of Varosha**, once one of the **most popular resorts in Europe**. The crystal-clear waters and abandoned luxury hotels stand as a haunting reminder of a glamorous past. **Famous celebrities like Richard Burton, Elizabeth Taylor, and Brigitte Bardot** once vacationed here.

A drive around the **gothic city** will then take us to the **fortified walls of Famagusta**, where you will have **free time** to explore historical landmarks, including:

  • ✔ **Latin Church of St. George & Greek Church of St. George**
  • ✔ **Arsenal (Martinengo Bastion)**
  • ✔ **Othello Castle (Venetian Governor’s Castle)**
  • ✔ **St. Peter & St. Paul Church**
  • ✔ **St. Nicolas Cathedral (now Lala Mustafa Pasha Mosque)**
  • ✔ **Nestorian Church of St. George & remains of St. Francis Church**
  • ✔ **The Venetian Palace ruins**

You can **browse the local shops**, grab a unique souvenir, or simply sit back and enjoy something to eat from a wide selection of **exotic food and sweets** before heading back.

"Explore the famous landmarks of Ayia Napa"

An in-depth **off-road adventure** to the **most Eastern point** of the East Coast National Park, covering the most famous landmarks of **Ayia Napa & Protaras**.

We begin the tour with our first stop at the **natural bridge in Ayia Napa**, famously known as the **“Lovers Bridge”**. Then, we take the off-road trail to reach the **famous Sea Caves**, a popular destination for those brave enough to dive into the **crystal-clear waters**.

Our journey continues to the **top of Cape Greko**, where a **10-minute trek** rewards us with **360° panoramic views** of the Mediterranean Sea.

Next, we visit the **natural bridge of Koraka** for some breathtaking photos before heading to the **hidden cave** that was once used as a **church**. Above this cave stands the **blue and white chapel of Agioi Anargoiroi**, a popular spot for dreamlike summer weddings.

Our next stop is the **stunning Konnos Bay**, where you’ll have free time to soak in the picturesque scenery. We continue to the **Mythical Cave of Cyclopes** and end our adventure at the **unique little church of Ayioi Saranta**, a secluded and hidden gem that even locals struggle to find.

Before wrapping up our adventure, we will visit the **Ayia Napa Sculpture Park**, which houses countless sculptures donated by artists from around the world in the name of **peace**. This park stands as one of the most important **cultural landmarks in Cyprus**, offering stunning photography opportunities.

A memorable off-road adventure for the whole family that will leave you with **unforgettable memories!** 🌍✨

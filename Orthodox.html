Pilgrimage to Mount Athos

Day 1 Meeting at the airport of Thessaloniki. Transfer to the hotel in Ouranoupolis. Accommodation at the hotel (50 Euro supplement for 1 bed accommodation). Dinner in the tavern at your own expense (20-30 euros).

Day 2 Together with the group, receive diamonetirion and buy ferry tickets. Departure to the Skete of St. Anne, climbing to the top and veneration of the miraculous icon of St. Anna and her incorrupt foot. Walking to the Monastery of St. Paul. Evening service, veneration of the shrines of the monastery, including the Gifts of the Magi. Meal. Overnight in the monastery.

Day 3 Liturgy and meal. Transfer by ferry to the Monastery of Dionysios. Veneration of the miraculous Akathist Icon (created by St. Luke), the right hand of John the Baptist and the relics of St. Paraskeva. Transfer to Grigoriat Monastery. Evening service, veneration of the shrines of the monastery. Meal. Overnight stay.

Day 4 Liturgy and meal. Departure by ferry to the port of Dafni. Transfer to the bus, transfer to Karyes (the capital of Athos), veneration of the icon "It is truly meet". Transfer to the skete of St. Andrew the First-Called. Worship of His incorruptible head. Trekking to Koutloumoumous Monastery, veneration of shrines. Transfer to the monastery of Iviron. Evening service. Veneration of the Icon of the Mother of God Portaitissa (Iveron). Meal. Overnight stay.

Day 5 Liturgy and meal. Transfer to the Monastery of Philotheus. Accommodation in the monastery and a walk to the Caracal Monastery. Veneration of the shrines of the monastery. Return to the Monastery of Philotheus. Evening service, veneration of shrines. Meal. Overnight stay.

Day 6 Liturgy and meal. Transfer to Dochiar Monastery. Veneration of the miraculous icon "Quick to Hearken". Trekking to the Xenophon Monastery. Veneration of the shrines of the monastery. Transfer to the Monastery of St. Panteleimon. Evening service. Meal. Overnight stay.

Day 7 Liturgy and meal. Veneration of the head of the Great Martyr Panteleimon and the shrines of the monastery. Departure to Ouranoupolis. Accommodation at the hotel. A meal at your own expense in the tavern.

Day 8 Breakfast at the hotel. Free time, buying souvenirs. Transfer to the airport.

The cost is 740 euros.

Prior permission to visit Athos, meals in monasteries according to the monastic rule, escort around Athos, individual transfer for the group airport-Ouranoupolis-airport. Accommodation in the hotel in Ouranoupolis for the first and last night in a single, double or triple room. Ferry tickets Ouranoupolis-Athos-Ouranoupolis.

air travel, insurance, visa, stamp duty (25 euros for obtaining a diamonetiron), state tax, which is paid upon check-in (3 euros per room). Transfers by ferries and buses within Athos (about 40-50 euros). Personal expenses. Note: Overnight accommodation in monastery hotels on Mount Athos does not imply specific accommodation. Overnight stays in the monasteries indicated in the program are not guaranteed! If the monastery is unable to accept the group for the night on the date specified in the program, the group will be accommodated in another monastery.

Foreign pilgrimages

Pilgrimage to GEORGIA

PROGRAM

Day 1 Arrival. Meeting, transfer to the hotel.
Tbilisi. Sioni Cathedral: St. John of Manglisi (18th century), St. Martyr Catholicos Kirion III (20th century), St. Confessor Catholicos Ambrose of Khelaia (20th century), Apostle Thomas (head), cross of St. Equal-to-the- Apostles Nina. Patriarchal Holy Trinity Cathedral (Sameba): St. Confessor Gabriel of Samtavro (20th century); Stone of Grace (in the altar). Metekhi Church: Martyr Shushanika (5th century). Anchiskhati Church: St. Martyr Eudemona (17th century). Church of St. Equal-to-the- Apostles Nina: St. Confessor Stephen of Bodbe (20th century) (grave near the church). Russian Alexander Nevsky Church: St. Zinovy of Tetri- Tskaroy (20th century), "Smolensk" icon of the Mother of God, Schema- Archim. Vitaly (Sidorenko) (venerated grave near the church). Pantheon on Mount Mtatsminda: graves of the righteous Ilia Chavchavadze, martyr Dimitry Kipiani (19th century), righteous Euthymius Takaishvili (20th century). State Museum of the History of Fine Arts of Georgia named after Sh. Amiranashvili: originals of the "Khakul", "Atskur" and "Tsilkan" icons of the Mother of God, the "Anchiysky Spas" icon and the "Katskhi" icon of the Savior.

Day 2 Mtskheta.
Ascent to the Church of the Cross (Jvari), where Saint Nina, Equal to the Apostles, erected the cross. From here, there is a panoramic view of Mtskheta and the confluence of the Aragvi and Kura rivers. At the turn of the 6th-7th centuries, a cross-domed church was built over the Cross, as a kind of ark over the Cross. Unlike many other ancient churches, Jvari was never destroyed. This temple is a fragment of the country's very ancient past.
Svetitskhoveli Cathedral (4th century), under the lintel of which the Tunic of the Lord is buried. The mantle of the Prophet Elijah is walled up within the walls of the cathedral. A particle of the relics of the Apostle Andrew the First-Called is kept. A list of the miraculous Tsilkani Icon of the Mother of God (Healer).
Samtavro Temple, where the first Christian kings of Georgia are buried: Mirian and Nana. The temple was built by King Mirian on the site of a luxurious royal garden, where Saint Nina lived in a tent near a blackberry patch for many years and preached. In her memory, King Mirian built a chapel that has survived to this day. The temple contains the relics of many saints, including the miraculous relics of the holy father Gabriel Urgebadze, canonized on December 20, 2012.
Visit to the Shio-Mgvime Monastery. Founded sometime after 560 by Saint Shio, one of the Assyrian fathers, known as "Shio Mgvime".

Day 3 Kakheti region Mtskheta.
Bodbe Monastery of Saint Nina (we can plunge into the spring if we wish)
Sighnaghi, the city of love (a fortress city of Italian architecture. View of the Alazani Valley).
Monastery of Anthony of Markop (one of the elders of the 13 Assyrian Fathers).
Dinner

Day 4 Borjomi Resort.
Monastery of St. George in Chitakhevi ("Green Monastery") - in the Borjomi Gorge. People lovingly call it the "Green Monastery" because all the trees in the gorge are shades of green, as are the stones from which the monastery is built. Monastery of St. Seraphim of Sarov. Monastery of St. George the Victorious 7th century. Borjomi water sources in Borjomi Park. Warm sulfur baths in the open air in the Borjomi forest (you can take a dip if you wish).
Meeting of the cell attendant of St. Gabriel (Urgebadze) Mother Paraskeva. There will be an opportunity to touch the things of Fr. Gabriel (put on a skufia, mantle, epitrachelion of Father Gabriel, serve a prayer service).

Day 5 Kazbegi: Ananuri Temple 16th century place, glorified by A. Pushkin, M. Leromontov, A. Griboyedov. Source of the Terek, picturesque Darial Gorge, Mount Kazbek (5000 m). High-mountain temple Tsminda Sameba - Holy Trinity (12th century) in Gergeti. Miraculous icon of the Gergeti Mother of God, place of appearances of the Mother of God.

Day 6 IKALTO MONASTERY. Venerable Zeno (6th century) and Arseny (12th century) of Ikaltoy.
Medieval capital of Kakheti - Gremi fortress with the Church of the Archangels.
Alaverdi is a monastery and cathedral, officially the Cathedral of St. George in the Akhmeta region of Kakheti. Alaverdi Monastery is the creation of the Assyrian fathers.

Day 7 Walk through old Tbilisi. City market, dry bridge (souvenirs), Georgian feast (gastronomic tour).
Ethno House Mtkvaragvi (historical place where the Kura and Aragvi rivers join).
Shashlik on the vine, master class: cooking churchkhela, wine from Qvevri (two types of Saperavi and Rkatsiteli) and chacha (three types), Mengrelian khachapuri and Khinkali Mtiuluri, etc. and all this under the Georgian folklore "ODABADE" (1 person $ 30).
